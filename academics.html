Department of Artificial Intelligence And Data Science

Why IGCE is One of the Best Engineering Colleges in Tiruchirappalli & Tamil Nadu for AI Data Science?

The Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AI & DS) Department at Indra Ganesan College of Engineering (IGCE) commenced in June 2022, offering a specialized B.Tech program designed to impart strong foundations in statistics, mathematics, AI, machine learning, and data analytics. The curriculum is multidisciplinary, incorporating subjects like Big Data Analytics, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Pattern Recognition, and applied areas such as Digital Forensics and Natural Language Processing to help students build real-world skills. The department is supported by a dedicated faculty team led by Dr.B.Suganya (HOD), comprising professors, associate and assistant professors, all committed to academic excellence, research, and preparing students for emerging careers in AI & DS.

Department Vision and Mission

Vision
To be a center of excellence in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science education, research, and innovation, empowering students to solve real-world challenges ethically, responsibly, and with societal impact.

Mission

  • To provide a rigorous, multidisciplinary curriculum covering foundational and advanced topics in AI, Machine Learning, Big Data, and Data Analytics, with hands-on learning and industry collaboration.
  • To foster research, innovation, and entrepreneurship by promoting projects, internships, and faculty-student collaborations.
  • To instill professional ethics, critical thinking, and effective communication skills, preparing graduates to excel in global and societal contexts.
  • To continuously evolve with emerging technologies and trends, ensuring that graduates are future-ready, adaptable, and socially conscious.

    • Programme Educational Objectives (PEOs)

    PEO1: Technical knowledge
    To foster our young graduates with cogent technical knowledge so as to make them employable.

    PEO2: Real-Time Applications
    To apply the acquired knowledge in the field of Mathematics, Science and Engineering in developing real-time projects.

    PEO 3: Design Ability
    To design a system, component or process to meet the desired needs within realistic constraints such as manufacturing, economy, environmental sustainability, social, health and safety .

    PEO 4: Ethics
    To prepare the students to become entrepreneurs with professional attitude in the broader ethical perspective.

    PEO 5: Life - Long Learning
    To craft curiosity among students for life-long learning through self-study

    Programme Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

    PSO 1: Solving real time problems
    To develop capability to identify, analyze and solve engineering problems in concern to mechanical engineering along with
    associated engineering streams.

    PSO 2: Pursue Professional career
    To bestow quality learning environment to pursue professional career in mechanical engineering with integrated knowledge

    PSO 3: Concentrating on skill development
    To enflame the students technical capabilities in engineering design process, intra and inter personnel, linguistic and higher level professional skills required in engineering.

    Placements

    We ensure outstanding placement opportunities for our AI students, helping them secure positions in renowned companies. Our graduates are highly sought after in industries such as automotive, aerospace, manufacturing, robotics, and energy. Through focused training, industry exposure, and personalized guidance, we prepare students to excel in their professional careers. Our department is recognized as one of the top mechanical colleges in Pondicherry, with high placement rates and excellent industry connections

    Achievements

    The Department of Artificial Intelligence And Data Science at IGCE has a track record of remarkable accomplishments in academics, research, and industry placements, demonstrating our dedication to excellence and innovation in engineering education. We continue to be featured top 3 in the Pondicherry mechanical colleges list for our academic success and industry-relevant curriculum.

    Faculty

    The Department of Artificial Intelligence And Data science faculty comprises seasoned professionals with a wide range of specializations and substantial academic backgrounds. They are committed to fostering student growth via quality education, research, and industry participation, guaranteeing a well-rounded learning experience for all. This solid foundation helps our department rank among the top 10 AI engineering colleges in Tamil Nadu.

    Infrastructure

    AI Laboratory

    This laboratory at IGCE’s AI department focuses on hands-on instruction in fluid mechanics and hydraulic machinery. It includes crucial equipment, including venturi metres, \ orifice metres, notches, weirs, and numerous pump configurations. These resources provide students with practical insights into the operation and principles of flow measurement and hydraulic machinery.
