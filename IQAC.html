Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)

IQAC

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) of Indra Ganesan College of Engineering (IGCE) functions as a pivotal body to ensure and enhance the overall quality of academic and administrative activities of the institution. The IQAC constantly works towards strengthening the quality framework through continuous assessment, feedback, and improvement processes. It promotes outcome-based education, encourages research and innovation, and ensures adherence to institutional policies and accreditation standards. By fostering a culture of excellence, the IQAC at IGCE strives to achieve sustained quality enhancement and to provide students with a holistic learning environment aligned with national and global educational standards.

Vision

To build and ensure a quality culture aimed at all round excellence in the educational practices, processes and methods.

Mission

  • Impact quality education to meet the needs of the profession and society.
  • Appeal and increase perpetrate human resource and provide an excellent innovation and research environment.
  • Facilitate network with alumni, business, industry and research organizations.
  • Enlighten the hidden technical skills and abilities of youth with highest standards of profession ethics, team spirit, transparency and entrepreneurial skills.

    • Functions & Responsibilities

    • To ensure continuous improvement in academic and administrative performance.
    • To promote research, innovation, and technology-driven learning.
    • To maintain quality standards in teaching, learning, and evaluation processes.
    • To enhance industry–institute interaction and encourage collaborative projects.
    • To support faculty development and student skill enhancement programs.
    • To monitor and evaluate institutional activities through regular quality assessments.
    • To prepare and maintain accreditation, audit, and compliance reports.
    • To encourage social responsibility, ethics, and sustainable campus development.
    • To create a conducive environment for holistic student growth and career readiness.
    • To strengthen institutional governance through transparency and accountability.
    • To align institutional objectives with national and global education standards.

    Benefits

    IQAC will ensure heightened level of clarity and focus in Institutional functioning leading towards quality enhancement

    IQAC will ensure internalization of the quality culture.

    IQAC will ensure enhancement and coordination among various activities of the Institution and institutionalize all good practices.

    IQAC Coordinator Message

    The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) of Indra Ganesan College of Engineering (IGCE) plays a vital role in promoting a culture of continuous improvement and academic excellence across all departments. As the IQAC Coordinator, I take immense pride in our institution’s commitment to quality enhancement through innovation, collaboration, and outcome-based education. The IQAC ensures that every academic and administrative process is aligned with the vision and mission of the college, focusing on developing competent, ethical, and socially responsible professionals.

    The IQAC of Indra Ganesan College of Engineering acts as a catalyst in institutional growth by integrating quality initiatives into every aspect of academic and administrative functioning. We consistently encourage faculty to adopt innovative teaching methods, undertake impactful research, and engage in professional development.

    The cell also emphasizes student-centric learning by promoting experiential education, entrepreneurship, and value-based activities. With collective effort and a vision for excellence, the IQAC continues to strengthen IGCE’s legacy as a center of learning, innovation, and societal contribution.

    Dr. M. Santhi
    Coordinator, IQAC

    Credentials

    • An autonomous institution approved by AICTE and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai.
    • Accredited by NAAC for excellence in academic quality and institutional performance.
    • Established under the Indra Ganesan Educational & Charitable Trust in 2008.
    • Recognized research centers in multiple engineering and technology disciplines.
    • Offers well-structured UG, PG, and Ph.D. programs across various departments.
    • Equipped with advanced laboratories, modern infrastructure, and digital classrooms.
    • Strong placement records with leading multinational and core companies.
    • Experienced faculty with strong academic and industry backgrounds.
    • Industry-collaborative curriculum designed for real-world application.
    • Active Memoranda of Understanding (MoUs) with top industries and universities.
    • Dedicated Research & Development Cell promoting innovation and patents.
    • Functional IQAC cell ensuring continuous quality enhancement.
    • Encourages student entrepreneurship through innovation and incubation centers.
    • Provides scholarships and financial assistance for deserving students.
    • Regularly conducts national-level seminars, workshops, and hackathons.
    • Promotes green campus initiatives and sustainability awareness programs.
    • Digitalized library with access to e-journals and global research databases.
    • Well-maintained hostel and transport facilities for student convenience.
    • Focus on outcome-based education and skill-oriented training.
    • Encourages student clubs and associations for holistic personality development.
    • Offers value-added courses and certification programs for employability enhancement.
    • Active participation in societal outreach and community development programs.
    • Provides continuous support for alumni networking and career progression.
    • Recognized for excellent academic results and university rankings.
    • Strives for continuous improvement through innovation, research, and quality education.
