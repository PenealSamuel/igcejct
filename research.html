About the IGCE Research & Development Cell

The Research & Development Cell of Indra Ganesan College of Engineering (IGCE) serves as a catalyst for innovation, creativity, and knowledge creation among students and faculty. The R&D Cell promotes a culture of research by encouraging interdisciplinary projects, collaborations with industries, and participation in funded research programs. It provides a platform for students and faculty to transform their innovative ideas into tangible outcomes through publications, patents, and product development. The Cell continuously works towards enhancing the research ecosystem of the institution, thereby contributing to the advancement of technology and societal development.

Vision

To create research culture among the faculties and students thereby nurturing innovation in Research and Development, Quality Publications, Consultancy, Patents and Entrepreneurial skills.

Mission

  • Impact quality education to meet the needs of the profession and society.
  • Appeal and increase perpetrate human resource and provide an excellent innovation and research environment.
  • Facilitate network with alumni, business, industry and research organizations.
  • Enlighten the hidden technical skills and abilities of youth with highest standards of profession ethics, team spirit, transparency and entrepreneurial skills.
